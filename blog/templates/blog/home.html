Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does this website work?

    Simply, after subscribing to one of the available packages, enter your phone number in the designated field and click the "Create Link" button. A unique link containing your phone number will be generated. You can copy this link and use it as desired.

  • Do customers need to download a specific app to use the link?

    No, customers do not need to download any specific app. When they click on the link, they will be directly redirected to their phone dialer to automatically add your number or call it.

  • Can I cancel the link after a specific period of time?

    Yes, you can cancel the link after a specific period of time. You can set an expiration date for the link, and after that, it will no longer be valid for use.

  • Can I use the link on social media platforms?

    Yes, you can use the link on social media platforms. You can share the link in your posts or tweets to enable direct communication with customers and increase conversion opportunities.
